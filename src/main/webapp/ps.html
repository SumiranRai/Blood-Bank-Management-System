Blood Donor:

A blood donor (a person who wants to donate blood) goes to a blood donation facility to donate his her blood. (A blood donation campaign , blood bank, hospital, medical clinic). The blood donor is questioned about his blood donation history and any allergies, symptoms or diseases he was/is suffering by the medical staff.

Donor's Details
Name, Age, Gender, Weight, Date of Donation, Blood Group, Quantity, Place (Hospital's name / Camp), Name of the medical staff, Donor's Id.


If the Donor is comming for the first time then a Donor's Id is provided for future records.



Blood collected is used for various purposes: Haemoglobin , WBCs , RBCs , Blood Platelets, Blood Plasma.

All of the various blood products may be kept in different blood bank facilities and may have different time of expiry. Blood bank contains blood products collected from different locations.

Product Info:
Product_id , product_name , blood_group_rh , donor_id , quantity_of_Product, place_of_collectionp, date_of_expiry.
Quantities of various products along with the locations must be noted. Blood and its products are used for various purposes. Therefore, blood from the blood bank must be utilized properly and as per the demand it needs to be transported for saving human lives. Address of the place to where the blood/product is being sent and also quantity of the product must be noted. Blood may be used by hospitals for operations/ medical purposes/ blood transfusion/ first-aid purposes.


Blood Recipient:

The Things noted of Receipient:
Name, Age, Gender, Weight, Date of Receiving, Blood Group, Blood Product, Quantity, Reason, Place (Hospital's name / Camp), Name of the medical staff, Receipient's Id.
